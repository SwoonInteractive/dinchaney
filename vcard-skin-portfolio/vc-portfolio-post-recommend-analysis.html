Recommender for Trending Products

A product recommendation engine is just about as essential as a shopping cart feature for online shops. Using algorithms and data, I built a simple yet powerful product recommender for trending products.
Higher conversion rates, increased revenues and customer retention are just a few of the benefits.

For the store owner, the engine also provides an insightful look into products that are starting to lose popularity, how newly added products are performing and valuable information on revenue - is it diversified or primarily one or two key products?
This data is also useful in determining which new products to add to inventory. The possibilities and value are endless.
